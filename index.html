About Me

In fall 2020, I will join Alibaba Group in Hangzhou, China, as a senior machine learning algorithm engineer.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Cornell University, Ithaca, NY, USA, in 2020. I was advised by Prof. Qing Zhao. Prior to that, I obtained my B.S. degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2015.

Research Interests

My research interests include statistical online learning and sequential decision theory with applications in various socio-economic systems and communication networks. I am also interested in the topic of recommendation systems, algorithmic game theory, and distributed learning.
